## 🔬 Research & Projects

### 🇩🇪 [German Small Language Model](https://github.com/AbhishekKange/german-slm)
Custom GPT-2 architecture trained from scratch on 200K translated German stories
- Implemented multi-head attention, layer normalization, and causal masking
- Explored cross-lingual transfer learning using NLLB translation
- **Tech:** PyTorch, Transformers, Custom Tokenizers

### 🎨 Diffusion Models (In Progress)
Implementing DDPM (Denoising Diffusion Probabilistic Models) from scratch
- Understanding the mathematical foundations of diffusion processes
- Training on custom datasets for image generation

### 🧮 Neural Networks from Scratch
Building fundamental architectures without frameworks
- Backpropagation, gradient descent, and optimization algorithms
- CNNs, RNNs, and Transformer components
